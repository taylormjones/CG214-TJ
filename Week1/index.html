Taylor Jones

TayJonesDesigns originated in Long Island, New York in the late 90's. An upcoming artist that started as a fashion designer to a full time Fine Arts finding her new horzion on the next chapters of her life. TayJones is a visual, realistic and contempory modenist. TayJones's inspiration comes from her everyday life and abstract complex world we know today.

Currently TayJones is in the process of working with ArtSpeak2017 at the Fashion Institute of Technology in mid April for the juniors in the Fine Arts BFA program. Jones's art pieces contain paintings of black America, being unapologetically black through black history.
