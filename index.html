by Google Data Arts Team April 19, 2016 Week04 As I observe throughhttps://www.chromeexperiments.com/experiment/virtual-art-sessions. I discovered these beautiful and aesthetic experiments. One that crossed me was the "Visual Art Sessions.

Behind the Scenes

Virtual Art Sessions were created by the Google Data Arts Team. In the process of the experiment they used a "Tilt Brush" which is used to create life size 3D brush strokes of different elements like a painting. The project partnered with 6 world renowned artists bringing them a new way to paint, draw and sculpt. We recorded their sessions and made it accessible online, letting anyone observe the artists as they develop their VR creations from any angle.

List of What to Expect from Virtual Art Sessions

1. Aesthetic and avante garde features 2. Vitural features and examples 3. Common goal for experiment 4.High Volumes of Dada included in the browser 4. Suspense and Creativity through drawing
